Planet-Positive

Study smart.
Save the planet.

Traditional paper flashcards have a hidden environmental cost. Every card you generate digitally is a card that doesn't consume water, cut down trees, or release CO₂.

💧
10 litres of water are consumed to produce a single A4 sheet of paper — confirmed by the Environment Agency and Water Footprint Network. A typical student creates hundreds of flashcards per year.
🌳
35% of all harvested trees globally are used for paper manufacturing. Choosing digital flashcards directly reduces demand for virgin wood pulp.
♻️
Paper production is energy-intensive, releasing CO₂ through deforestation, pulping, bleaching, and transport. A digital flashcard generates a fraction of that footprint.
🌱
At scale, the impact is enormous. If 100,000 students switch from paper to FlashDeck, they collectively save over 50 million litres of water per year — equivalent to 20 Olympic swimming pools.
🌍 The FlashDeck Sustainability Pledge

FlashDeck.AI is committed to carbon-neutral operations. We offset 100% of our server emissions through certified reforestation programmes, and we publish an annual environmental impact report. Every card generated is a step toward a greener future for education.

FAQ

Common questions
answered

Which exam boards does FlashDeck support? +
FlashDeck currently supports AQA, Edexcel (Pearson), Cambridge Assessment International Education (IGCSE & A Level), and OCR. Each board's specification is baked into the AI prompt, so cards are always relevant to your exact syllabus. IB Diploma support is coming soon.
How accurate are the AI-generated flashcards? +
FlashDeck uses Claude AI, one of the most accurate large language models available, trained on extensive academic content. Cards are generated with exam board context built in. As with any AI tool, we recommend cross-referencing with your textbook for critical topics — and we make this easy with source references on each card.
Can I use FlashDeck outside the UK? +
Absolutely. FlashDeck is designed for global use. Cambridge IGCSE is taught in over 140 countries, and our platform works on any device with an internet connection. We also offer geo-pricing for students in qualifying low-income countries, making FlashDeck accessible worldwide.
What subjects are available? +
At launch, FlashDeck covers all core GCSE and A Level sciences (Biology, Chemistry, Physics), Mathematics, and key humanities (History, Geography, English Literature). Additional subjects are being added continuously. If your subject isn't listed, contact us — we can often add it quickly.
Is my data private and secure? +
Yes. FlashDeck is built on Supabase (enterprise-grade PostgreSQL) with row-level security. We never sell user data. All connections are encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. We are compliant with UK GDPR and COPPA for under-18 users. Full privacy policy available at launch.
How does FlashDeck help the environment? +
Every digital flashcard replaces a paper one that would have consumed approximately 10 litres of water and contributed to CO₂ emissions through paper manufacturing. At scale, this is significant: 100,000 students switching to FlashDeck saves over 50 million litres of water annually. FlashDeck also offsets 100% of server carbon emissions through certified reforestation programmes.
