Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

Is FlashDeck GDPR compliant?
Yes. FlashDeck is built with GDPR compliance at its core. We offer a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) for all school accounts. Student data is never sold or used for advertising.
Does it require any IT setup?
No. FlashDeck runs entirely in the browser — no software to install, no firewall changes needed. Students access it on any device with an internet connection.
Which exam boards are supported?
FlashDeck currently supports AQA, Edexcel (Pearson), Cambridge Assessment International Education (IGCSE & A Level), and OCR. Each board's specification is built into the AI, so cards are always relevant to the exact syllabus. Additional boards are on the roadmap — contact us if your board isn't listed.
Can teachers control what students see?
Yes. Teachers can assign specific topics, set recommended decks, and monitor which content students are generating — all from the teacher dashboard.
What happens after the pilot?
At the end of the 6-week pilot, we'll share an engagement report and discuss whether a paid plan is right for your school. There's no obligation to continue.
Is there a minimum commitment?
No minimum commitment for the pilot. Paid plans are billed annually. We also offer monthly billing for smaller institutions — ask us for details.
Can we get a DPA before the pilot?
Absolutely. We can provide a Data Processing Agreement before the pilot begins. Email us at hello@flashdeck.ai and we'll send it over promptly.
Do you offer MAT or multi-school pricing?
Yes. We offer discounted pricing for Multi-Academy Trusts and groups of schools. Contact us to discuss a bespoke arrangement.
